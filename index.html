     MY BACKGROUND     

Born and raised in a beautiful coastal city of Split , I began my culinary career working for Mr Marzotto aboard the Yacht "Mavra",
Sailing mainly on the Adriatic and Mediterranean seas.
When there was nothing left for me to learn aboard Mavra , I decided to test myself in restaurant business,
In my long career i have worked for many famous restaurants, Including Michelin starred Enoteca Pinchiorri.
As the time went by , I couldnt shake the thought of opening my own place, My very own Restaurant.!
I have finally opened my own Restaurant business in Germany, in the early 2000's followed by another in Croatia.
After roughly two successful decades of working and running a restaurant business, I felt as if the sea was calling once more, roughly fifteen years to this day. I have decided to work aboard a yacht once more.
